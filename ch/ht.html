MUNICIPALITY PROBLEMS

Be it overflowing bins , stray dog menace or breeding mosquitoes in your locality , you have to deal with them till the authority takes an action. Only when people become ill or are attacked by dogs, and the issue becomes a headline in the newspaper some action is taken.
But isn't it too late. We keep on posting on social media about the negligence of our authorities , mocking them and gaining sympathies . Now its time to present your problems at a platform where we aim to bring together the world to share their problems and call for proper action. Becaues its rightly said
'united we stand divided we fall'.

LAW AND ORDER

We often wish that we were born in some other country. Smooth flowing traffics , safer roads etc. So lets get together to make Indian roads safer. We might change ourselves and follow the rules.
But one person cannot change the entire country. Post pictures of people breaking the rules. Not only traffic rules but other rules as well which are considered to result in punishment if broken. If you see someone littering the roads, peeing in public,damaging property,indulging in theft etc. Post pictures , details etc so that action can be taken against them.

SOCIETY UPLIFTMENT

A part of our society is developed but the rest of it still struggling.We cannot grow if we dont grow together. People living in slums or villages are not equipped enough for fighting for themselves. They not only lack resources but are far behind in accessing the latest technologies.So be a voice for them.Put forth their problems. So that the government can take proper actions for their upliftment.We can make use of this technology available to us for doing so much of social work. With this aim muddlefix has been developed.
