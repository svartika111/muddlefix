FAQ's

Questions?Get solutions Here!

What type of problems can be addressed?

You can address all kinds of problems related to municipality, society problems etc. For more information click on PROBLEM tab to get detailed description

Where to make submissions?

On the top you will find a FORM option. Click on it. Write about your problem and give a description of it.

How will the problems be solved?

Solution to the problem can be found if we know the type of problem. Once you submit your problem accordingly we'll help you to find a solution. We shall contact the concerned authorities. Once we get a fair idea of what kinds of problem are to be addressed accordingly, we will get our platform recognised so that we can provide quick official access.

How will publicly presenting my problem help?

A single person is not affected by some issue. Its the masses which suffer. But all do not turn up and neither we can get them. So our platform allows people to unite against a problem. United we stand, divided we fall is our motto.

What is the aim of MuddleFix?

We aim to develop a community with minimized problems and better living standards. We want that you don't feel alone when it comes to facing any problem.
